Virtual Time Capsule Steps

Create a Time Capsule

Users sign in to their accounts or create new ones on your platform. They initiate the creation of a time capsule, providing necessary details like title, description, and choosing the opening date.

Uploading Contents

Users can upload various types of content such as text messages, images, videos, documents, etc., that they wish to include in the capsule.

Locking the Capsule in Blockchain

At this point, your platform generates a unique identifier (NFT) for this time capsule. A smart contract is then deployed on the Ethereum blockchain, which holds the details of the time capsule (NFT ID, opening date, contents, etc.).

Securing the Capsule

The smart contract enforces the time lock. It ensures that the capsule cannot be accessed until the specified date arrives.

Storage on Blockchain

The contents of the time capsule, along with the details, are securely stored on the Ethereum blockchain. This provides tamper-proof and immutable storage.

Confirmation and Receipt

Users receive a confirmation along with the unique NFT identifier for their time capsule. This serves as proof of ownership.

Monitoring and Editing

Users can log in to their accounts to view, edit, or monitor the status of their capsules. They can also receive notifications as the chosen release date approaches.

Unlocking on Opening Date

When the chosen date arrives, the smart contract automatically executes, allowing the user to access the contents of their time capsule.

Optional: Sale of Capsules

Users have the option to make their capsules available for sale. They can set a price or choose to offer it for free.
